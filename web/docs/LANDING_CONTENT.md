## Content Principles Applied

1. **Every headline is under 10 words.** No exceptions.
2. **Every body paragraph is under 40 words.** Tight. Scannable.
3. **Second person throughout.** "Your family", "you upload", "your father's test."
4. **No jargon.** "Lab report" not "medical document." "Blood sugar" not "glucose panel."
5. **Lime highlights mark the most important phrase** in each major headline — it's a visual reading guide.
6. **Overlines on every section** — creates rhythm, aids scanning, adds typographic texture.
